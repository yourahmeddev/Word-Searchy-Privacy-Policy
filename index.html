Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 10, 2025

Welcome to WordSearchy. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

Summary: WordSearchy is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information. The game works entirely offline and does not require an internet connection to play.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. WordSearchy is a standalone word puzzle game that operates entirely on your device. We do not:

  • Collect your name, email address, or contact information
  • Track your location
  • Access your contacts, photos, or other personal files
  • Collect device identifiers for tracking purposes
  • Use analytics or tracking services
  • Require account registration or login

2. Data Storage

All game data, including your progress, settings, and preferences, is stored locally on your device. This data includes:

  • Game progress and completed puzzles
  • User preferences (theme selection, difficulty level)
  • High scores and statistics

This information never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third parties.

3. Children's Privacy

WordSearchy is safe for users of all ages, including children under 13. Since we do not collect any personal information, our app complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations worldwide.

We do not:

  • Knowingly collect personal information from children
  • Include features that could identify or track children
  • Display targeted advertisements
  • Include links to external websites or social media

4. Third-Party Services

WordSearchy does not integrate any third-party services that collect user data. We do not use:

  • Third-party analytics tools
  • Advertising networks
  • Social media integrations
  • Cloud storage services

5. Internet Connection

WordSearchy does not require an internet connection to function. The game works completely offline. Any network permissions, if present, are solely for optional features like checking for app updates through the official app store.

6. Data Security

Since all data remains on your device and we do not collect or transmit any information, there is no risk of data breach from our end. Your game data is as secure as your device itself.

7. Your Rights

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no personal information to access, modify, or delete. If you wish to remove all game data, you can simply uninstall the application from your device.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

9. App Permissions

WordSearchy may request the following permissions:

  • Storage (optional): Only if needed to save game data locally
  • Vibration (optional): For haptic feedback during gameplay

We only request permissions that are essential for the app's core functionality.
